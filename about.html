About Awad Insurance Agency

Awad Insurance Agency is a locally owned, AAA-affiliated insurance agency located at 15201 Dix Toledo Road in Southgate, Michigan. We provide personalized auto, home, life, business, umbrella, and inland flood insurance to families and businesses throughout the Downriver area.

Our Story

We opened our doors in Southgate because we believe our neighbors deserve better than a 1-800 number. Insurance is personal — it protects your family, your home, your livelihood — and we think the people who help you choose that protection should know your name.

As an AAA-affiliated agency, we bring together the strength of one of America's most trusted brands with the care and attention of a local, independent office. When you walk through our door or give us a call, you're talking to someone who lives and works in this community — not a script reader in a distant call center.

What We Stand For

Personalized Service

When you call Awad Insurance, you talk to a real person who knows your policy and your name. We take the time to understand your situation and find coverage that actually fits.

Honest Advice

We don't push products you don't need. As an independent agency, we work for you — not for one insurance company. Our recommendations are based on what's best for your family, not a sales quota.

Claims Advocacy

When something goes wrong, we're in your corner. We help you navigate the claims process, communicate with the carrier on your behalf, and make sure you get what you're owed.

Community First

We live and work in Southgate. The families and businesses we protect are our neighbors. That's not a marketing line — it's why we do this.

Insurance Products We Offer

We provide comprehensive insurance solutions for individuals, families, and businesses in Southgate and across the Downriver area:

Auto Insurance

Michigan no-fault expertise, competitive rates from multiple carriers, and help choosing the right PIP coverage level for your family.

Home Insurance

Protect your home against Michigan weather, theft, liability, and more. We help you find the right balance of coverage and affordability.

Life Insurance

Term life, whole life, and universal life options to protect your family's financial future. We help you determine how much coverage you need.

Business Insurance

General liability, commercial auto, workers' compensation, and business owner's policies for Downriver small businesses.

Umbrella Insurance

Extra liability protection beyond your auto and home policies — essential for homeowners, landlords, and anyone with assets to protect.

Inland Flood Insurance

Standard homeowners policies don't cover flooding. We offer inland flood coverage to protect your home from water damage — a real risk in Michigan.
