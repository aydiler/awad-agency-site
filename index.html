Insurance for Southgate & Downriver Michigan

Awad Agency is a locally owned, AAA-affiliated insurance agency located at 15201 Dix Toledo Road in Southgate. We help individuals, families, and businesses find the right coverage at competitive rates — because your peace of mind is our priority.

As an independent agency, we compare policies from multiple top-rated carriers to get you the best combination of coverage and price. Whether you need auto, home, life, business, or umbrella insurance, we'll build a plan that fits your needs and budget.

Insurance Services We Offer

We provide a full range of personal and commercial insurance products tailored to Michigan residents.

Auto Insurance

Michigan no-fault expertise, competitive rates, and AAA roadside assistance. We help you navigate PIP coverage levels and find the best price.

Home Insurance

Protect your home against Michigan weather, theft, liability, and more. We cover dwelling, personal property, sewer backup, and additional structures.

Life Insurance

Term and whole life policies to protect your family's financial future. We help you determine the right coverage amount for your situation.

Business Insurance

General liability, workers' compensation, commercial property, and BOP policies. Coverage for Michigan small businesses and contractors.

Umbrella Insurance

Extra liability protection beyond your auto and home policy limits. Affordable coverage that protects your assets and future earnings.

Need Something Else?

Renters, flood, recreational vehicles, and more. Contact us to discuss your coverage needs — we'll find the right policy for you.
